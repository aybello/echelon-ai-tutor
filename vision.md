## What Echelon Is

Echelon Institute (echeloninstitute.ca) is a SaaS platform that helps Canadian regulated trade professionals prepare for and pass their provincial certification exams. The platform delivers province-specific practice questions, an AI tutor that explains every wrong answer, adaptive difficulty, flashcards, timed mock exams, and a student performance dashboard.

The current product covers two certification frameworks:
- **Ontario:** OWWCO/MECP (Ontario Water and Wastewater Certification Office) — Classes 1 through 4 across Water Treatment and Wastewater Treatment streams
- **Western Canada:** WPI (Water and Wastewater Operators Certification Program) — Classes I through IV across Water Treatment, Wastewater Treatment, Water Distribution, and Wastewater Collection tracks, covering BC, AB, SK, and MB

## Competitive Moat

1. **Content depth:** 15,737 questions took 12 months to build and audit — cannot be replicated quickly
2. **Regulatory specificity:** Questions are mapped to Ontario Reg 128/04 and WPI Need-to-Know Criteria — generalist AI tools cannot match this
3. **AI tutor quality:** Claude-powered explanations that understand the specific regulatory context
4. **Province-specific:** Not US-focused like most competitors (Water Operator, ABC)
5. **Subscription model:** Operators study for 6-18 months before exams, creating natural long-term retention
